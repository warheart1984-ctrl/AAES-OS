## 3. Constitutional meaning

CIS Core remains the sole authoritative constitutional source of normative requirements.

Companion specifications shall inherit CIS Core terminology and shall not redefine constitutional terms.

All downstream specifications, runtime artifacts, and evidence surfaces must remain traceable to this frozen baseline.

## 4. Change control

Any future change to frozen CIS Core terminology, requirements, or constitutional scope must proceed through the governed constitutional change process, including CCR and CCP controls where applicable.

No informal agreement, implementation detail, or downstream profile may alter the frozen constitutional baseline.

## 5. Relationship to companion specifications

Companion specifications may evolve independently, but they must continue to trace back to this frozen constitutional source.

The freeze does not transfer constitutional authority to any companion specification, runtime implementation, or profile.
