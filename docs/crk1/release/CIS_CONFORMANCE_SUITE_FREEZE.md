**Release family:** CRK-1 companion specification  
**Status:** Frozen  
**Effective date:** 2026-07-12

## 1. Freeze declaration

The CIS Conformance Suite Specification is hereby designated **Frozen** and constitutes a frozen companion baseline for the CIS ecosystem.

This freeze establishes the normative verification surface for constitutional, replay, receipt, trust, and acceptance validation.

## 3. Constitutional meaning

The conformance suite remains subordinate to CIS Core v1.0.

The suite shall verify constitutional obligations without redefining them and shall treat the traceability matrix as the source of requirement coverage.

## 4. Change control

Any future change to the suite's terminology, validation rules, or acceptance criteria must proceed through the governed constitutional change process.

No implementation detail or informal agreement may alter the frozen conformance-suite baseline.
