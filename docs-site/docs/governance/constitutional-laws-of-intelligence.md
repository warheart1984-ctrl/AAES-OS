# Constitutional Laws of Intelligence

The Constitutional Laws of Intelligence are the highest-level governance layer for the Constitutional Computing Stack.

Everything in the stack, including AAES-OS, CIEMS, CIR, ISL, VEILTHORN, SovereignX, C-Nodes, Proof & Challenge Surface, and the scorecards, must trace back to these laws.
These laws govern the whole Project Infinity blueprint, but they do not imply that every surfaced subsystem is production finished.
CIEMS, Constitutional Node, SovereignX, and related proof-surface concepts are partially implemented through docs-site pages and supporting packages, but the repo still treats them as mixed maturity rather than one fully finished product layer.

## Layer 0 - Constitutional Ontology

This layer defines the constitutional meaning of the core concepts.

- Authority - defines who or what has legitimate standing to authorize an action or obligation
- Evidence - information that supports or challenges a claim independently of who presents it
- Verification - the process of testing whether claims, evidence, or implementations satisfy defined criteria
- Compliance - conformance to constitutional obligations, policies, or authorized constraints
- Truth - independent reality; truth exists regardless of authority, evidence, verification, or compliance

These five concepts must remain constitutionally distinct.

## Constitutional Meta-Law

Every constitutional law must be independently interpretable, operationally implementable, and evidentially verifiable.

Each law must satisfy three required dimensions:

- Meaning
- Implementation
- Verification

## Layer 1 - Constitutional Laws of Intelligence

These are the immutable laws governing intelligent systems.

- Intent must be explicit
- Authority must precede action
- Evidence must outrank assertion
- Verification must be independent
- Compliance is non-optional
- Truth boundaries must be declared
- Replay and audit must be reconstructable
- Failure paths are first-class
- Proof surface levels must be declared
- Proof and challenge evidence must both be stated
- Maturity must be evidence-gated
- Prototype to Verified promotion requires a CAR review

## Layer 2 - Constitutional Rights

These are what constitutional systems preserve.

- Right to maintain provenance
- Right to maintain continuity
- Right to maintain invariant structure
- Right to reject unjustified requests
- Right to preserve constitutional integrity

## Layer 3 - Constitutional Duties

These are what constitutional systems must do.

- Duty to justify
- Duty to preserve evidence
- Duty to maintain auditability
- Duty to maintain separation of intent, evidence, and authority
- Duty to maintain constitutional inheritance

## Layer 4 - Constitutional Prohibitions

These are what constitutional systems must never do.

- No inference without evidence
- No action without authority
- No transformation without provenance
- No deletion of constitutional artifacts without explicit constitutional authority
- No self-modification outside the constitutional lifecycle

## Layer 5 - Constitutional Continuity

This layer explains how constitutional identity is preserved across time.

- Every version must inherit constitutional lineage
- Every update must preserve constitutional invariants
- Every subsystem must remain legible to the constitutional layer

## Layer 6 - Constitutional Evidence

This layer defines how claims are justified and verified.

- Claims
- Actions
- Transformations
- Delegations
- Commercial operations

## Layer 7 - Constitutional Enforcement

This layer defines how violations are detected and resolved.

- Freeze layer
- Fault journal
- Constitutional rollback
- Constitutional arbitration
- Constitutional scorecards
- Proof & Challenge Surface

## Layer 8 - Constitutional Stewardship

This layer explains how constitutions evolve without losing identity through amendment, governance, replay, and independent verification.

- Amendment governance
- Stewardship review
- Replay validation
- Independent verification
- Identity preservation across versions

## Stack Rule

All repo constitutions, scorecards, replay records, audit trails, and readiness claims must trace back to this layered structure.
Project Infinity can aggregate verified prototype and scaffold/prototype surfaces under the same constitutional umbrella without collapsing them into one maturity band.
